Prana — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

Prana is a guided breathwork app built by Thorbjørn Bonvik. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data stored on your device

All session history, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

Prana displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics and tracking

Beyond what is collected by the ad provider, Prana does not include any additional analytics SDKs or tracking frameworks. No personal data is collected, shared, or sold to third parties by the developer.
