Stronq — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Stronq is a hypertrophy training app built by Thorbjørn Bonvik. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data stored on your device

All workout data, program progress, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Apple Health

With your permission, Stronq can read and write data to Apple Health:

  • Bodyweight entries you log may be saved to Health.
  • Completed workouts may be saved as exercise records.

Health data stays on your device and in iCloud (if you have Health syncing enabled). Stronq never sends Health data to any external service.

Purchases

Premium purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Stronq does not process or store any payment information.

Analytics and tracking

Stronq does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking frameworks, or advertising. No data is collected, shared, or sold to third parties.

Notifications

If you enable workout reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No push notification service or external server is involved.
